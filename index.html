About
US

Skilledshark was founded in 2020 with the mission of helping people live their best financial lives. We provide education and resources on a variety of topics, including credit repair, debt management and financial goal setting.
Our goal is to empower our people to make informed decisions about their finances, so they can achieve their short-term and long-term financial goals.
Skilledshark is headquartered in Melbourne, VIC and our team is made up of financial experts from a variety of backgrounds. We’re committed to providing unbiased information and personal support to help you reach your financial goals.
If you’re looking for help with your finances, we encourage you to browse our site and take advantage of our resources. If you have any questions or need assistance, please don’t hesitate to contact us. We’re here to help you achieve your financial dreams!
